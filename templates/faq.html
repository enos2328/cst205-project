Welcome! Have some questions?

Here is where the most frequently asked questions can be answered. If there is a question that you want to be answered, scroll to the very bottom and feel free to contact us for the answer!


Do I have to create an account?

No, you do not need to create an account to be able to use the app. The app is free to use to any who wishes to find the perfect recipe. However, you will need to make an account if you want to save any of the recipes that you have found. The account's purpose is only so that the recipes can be saved. You can also make your own recipes to add to your saved recipes.

Can I save my favorite recipes?

Yes, you can save your favorite recipes, but you will need to create an account to do so.

How can I save a recipe?

Simply click on the save button of the specified recipe you wish to save. Then, the next time you log in you will be able to access all the recipes.

Can I add my own recipes?

Yes, you can add your own recipes to your account. However, only users who have an account can add their own recipes.

Can I organize my saved recipes by category?

No, you can't organize your recipes by category. All the saved recipes for your account will be displayed in a single list.

Can I search recipes by name? (ex: lasagna, chicken)

Yes, you can search recipes by the recipe title. If you choose to search by name you will get multiple results with different variations that match your search request.
