Welcome! Have some questions?

Here is where the most frequently asked questions can be answered. If there is a question that you want to be answered, scroll to the very bottom and feel free to contact us for the answer!

Do I have to create an account?

No, you do not need to create an account to use the app. However, you will need to make an account if you want to save any of the recipes.

Can I save my favorite recipes?

Yes, if you want to save your favorite recipes you need to create an account.

How can I save a recipe?

Simply click on the save button. Next time you log in you will be able to access all the recipes.

Can I add my own recipes?

Yes, only users who have an account can add their own recipes.

Can I organize my saved recipes by category?

No, all your saved recipes will displayed in a single list

Can I search recipes by name? (ex: lasagna, chicken, )

Yes, if you choose to search by name you will get multiple results that match your request.
